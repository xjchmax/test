Message from the Dean

Yang Guanbin
Dean School of International Studies

As the People’s Republic of China reaches an important milestone of its 70th anniversary, School of International Studies, Renmin University of China, one of the oldest and top university-based faculty specialises in international relations and political science in PRC, is proud of its long term intellectual contribution to Chinese political development, foreign policy, and international cooperation. In the last 70years, reflecting the evolving interaction between China and the rest of the world, we have expanded our teaching and research from diplomacy and world socialism studies into Chinese politics, comparative politics, political theory, international.security, strategic studies, international political economy, and a broad range of regional studies. Generations of our graduates have devoted themselves to public and private sectors that drive the rise of China, academia and media that inform the public, and international organizations that bridge the globe. Besides working with our students on campus, our faculty members also transform their research into policy outputs, advice government agencies and shape public opinions.

The new era of world politics and China’s own development lead to both pressure and opportunities for SIS. Upgrading our academic disciplines, such as international relations and political science, calls for more innovation. We have to address the limitations of individual disciplines with further integration of international relations, political science, and other relevant disciplines within the framework of world politics studies. Due to China’s unprecedented engagement with the world, we have to broaden our vision from Western-centric focus to a truly global one.

We are prepared to deepen and broaden our international exchanges and cooperation, and Renmin University’s unique association with contemporary China, and our first class research on Chinese politics and foreign policy ensure that we can facilitate understanding of China by outsiders. In teaching, we provide more international exchange opportunities to our students, offer a wide range of degree programmes taught in Chinese and English to international students. Our international engagement also goes beyond ivory tower to organise or participate in discussion with international practitioners of our subjects, including politicians, diplomats, officers, and think thank analysts on shared interest.

Based on our predecessors’ academic achievement, with the continued support of our faculty members, students, alumni, partners and colleagues at home and abroad, we will make long term efforts to push SIS RUC toward a world class school of international relations and political science, contribute more to the discipline we focus on, provide more support to China’s own development, promote mutual understanding and confidence among different countries, as well as global governance, stability, and prosperity.
