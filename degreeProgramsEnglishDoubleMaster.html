Double Masters in Asian & International Affairs

About the program

Co-established by the School of International Studies, Renmin University of China (SISRUC) and the Department of European and International Studies, King’s College London, the Double MA is a two-year program in which students spend the first year studying Asian affairs at SISRUC and the second year studying International affairs at KCL. After completing the requirements in both universities, the students will be awarded respective degrees from two of the world’s most prestigious universities.

RUC-KCL Double MA recruits both local and international students, fostering a unique and global environment in which diverse backgrounds and experiences enhance rigorous academic study.

As leading teaching and research bases in the fields of political science and international studies in China and the UK, Renmin University and King’s College London offer students the opportunity to gain in-depth knowledge of Asian, European and global politics, economics, and society.

While living and studying in two of the world’s most dynamic and exciting cities of Beijing and London – each with their own vibrant academic communities – our young scholars from around the globe can work together to expand their experience, knowledge, and insights, with the overall goal of contributing to better mutual understanding between Asia and Europe.
