Organizational Structure

Academic Organization

Within the School of International Studies, there are seven departments and institutes responsible for the cultivation of undergraduates, postgraduates and doctoral students. These departments and institutes offer high-level teaching in several majors.

  • Department of Political Science
  • Department of International Politics
  • Department of Diplomacy
  • Institute of World Socialism Studies
  • Institute of Russian, Eastern European& Central Asian Studies
  • Institute of International Development Studies
  • Institute of Chinese Politics Studies
