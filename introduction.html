General Introduction

The School of International Studies of the Renmin University of China (SISRUC) is one of the oldest and top university-based schools specialising in international relations and political science in China. Our school is proud of its long-term intellectual contribution to China’s political development, foreign policies, and international cooperation. Since its foundation laid by Zhou Enlai, China’s then-Premier, we have expanded our teaching and research from diplomacy and world socialism studies into Chinese politics, comparative politics, political theory, international security, strategic studies, international political economy, and a broad range of area studies, closely resonating with the evolving interaction between China and the rest of the world.

  • public service

    Generations of our graduates have devoted themselves to serving public and private sectors driving the rise of China, academia and media informing the public, and international organizations connecting the world. Besides working closely with our students on campus, our faculty members also actively engage in transforming their research into policy outputs, advising government agencies and offering critical information and knowledge to the public.
