Academic Workshop on International Law Issues in China's Diplomacy

The "International Law Issues in China's Diplomacy" academic workshop was launched in September 2018 by the School of International Studies of Renmin University of China. The workshop aims to discuss international law issues in China's diplomacy in the new era and provide a legal perspective for China's multilateral diplomacy and international cooperation.

The work of this workshop includes:

  • (1)

    Expand academic contacts. From the conjoint perspective of diplomacy, international relations and international law, regular lectures and seminars are held to maintain academic vitality. The workshop aims to track the latest developments in this field, and accumulate resources for the research and teaching work.

  • (2)

    Promote academic output.Focusing on the subject of the workshop, the academic output will be combined with the construction of the workshop, and the academic achievements will be rewarded.

  • (3)

    Enrich personnel composition. To attract young teachers and excellent doctoral students into the workshop to enrich the academic research team of the workshop. At present, the main personnel of the workshop are: Pu Ping, Xu Ying, Zhu Xiaoqi, Du Xinlei, Zhou Yang, Yang Lulu and so on.
