Brief Introduction

About the program

The School of International Studies of Renmin University of China is delighted to offer students from our partner institutions (see the list below) the opportunity to study at Renmin for one semester or one academic year as exchange students. Each semester we welcome a new group of students who expect to not only explore their intellectual interest in international studies from a different perspective but also cultivate their understanding of China, one of the most dynamic countries, and its people while living in China‘s capital city.

Join our diverse community and make connections with students from all over the world！

Students from non-partner universities are equally welcome to join us through our visiting Program or the International Summer School Program. Details of the programs can be found here.
