Campus Life

Visa and Regulations

  • X1 visa
  • X2 visa

International students who plan tostudy in China for more than 6 months should apply for X1 visa to enter China.

X1 visa will be valid for only 30 days from the date you enter China. You need to apply for the Residence Permit within 10 days upon arrival in order to have legitimate identity in China.

The residence permit gives you multiple entries within the valid period.

Accommodation

You will receive your room application form together with your admission documents. In order to apply for accommodation on campus please follow the instructions on the form. Due to rising demand and limited resources, room allocation is based on a “first come, first served” principle. Therefore, international students who intend to live on-campus are advised to book their room as soon as they receive the admission package.

The Renmin University of China provides two types of dormitory rooms to the international students:

01

single room

RMB 80-100/day

private bedroom and bathroom

02

double room

RMB 31-40/day/person

two beds in one room, shared bathroom on each floor

Included in each room are bedclothes, air-conditioner, telephone, Internet access, and furniture.

Pictures of the dorms can be found here.
