Application Procedure

1.For students from mainland China, please find your application procedures in the Chinese prospectus.

2.For students holding the identity cards from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, please register with RUC Graduate School before December 15 of the previous year first and then apply online through KCL website.

3.For international students, please see your application procedures below:

(1)Apply online via KCL website.

(2)Once you get the offer from KCL, please also apply to Renmin University via the International Students Office application website. Should you plan to apply for the Chinese Government scholarship, we strongly encourage you to start your application early and to check the deadline with the relevant authority in your home country (usually the Chinese embassy/consulate).

(3)After your application documents are reviewed, a pre-admission letter will be digitally sent to you within two weeks. This pre-admission letter may serve you as proof of admission for any scholarship application in your home country.

(4)Official admission letters will be mailed to your postal address after June 15th. In the admission package you will find the admission letter for visa application and on-campus accommodation reservation form. The number of on-campus rooms is limited and cannot meet the demands of all students who want to live on campus. Therefore, international students who intend to live on-campus are advised to book their room as soon as they receive the admission package.

You may also refer to this outline for the details of the program application and scholarship application.
