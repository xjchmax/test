Degree Programs Taught in Chinese

The School of International Studies has been engaged in cultivating talents with a solid theoretical foundation, strong academic ability and world vision. The school remains one of the first institutions in China entitled to grant the doctoral degree in political science as a key disciplinary major at the national level. Currently, three majors are offered for undergraduate studies (4 years), nine for postgraduate (2 years) and PhD candidates (4 years) respectively.
