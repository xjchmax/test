Application

The School of International Studies of Renmin University of China is excited to greet new exchange students from our partner universities each year, who always enrich us with new ideas and bring diversity. 

  • Outstanding in academic performance and extracurricular activities
  • Interested in Political Science and International Studies
  • Passionate about China
  • Open-minded, proactive, and friendly
  • STEP01
    Nomination
  • STEP02
    Application
  • STEP03
    Admission

After receiving the application guidance, the nominated student completes the online application here with the following supportive documents:

The official transcripts
English version with the official stamp or signature, minimum GPA 3.0 out of 4.0 or average score 80 out of 100
The photocopy of passport
The passport must remain valid throughout the whole exchange period till six months after the period ends
The official certificate of enrollment
English version
The signed Guarantee Statement
please download the sample here
One recommendation letter
It can be from the Dean, Vice Dean, academic advisor, supervisor or professor who knows you well
The electronic ID picture
The picture must be in white background and in jpg format, between 100k to 500k. Please also name the picture by using the student’s passport number
