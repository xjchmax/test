Faculty Exchange

To foster high-level people exchanges, our school has established several programs and fellowships to allow international faculty to spend a period of time as lecturers in Beijing.

In the past, several US-based professors of the caliber of Prof. Peter J. Katzenstein (Cornell University), Prof. John J. Mearsheimer (Chicago University), Prof. Odd Arne Westad (Harvard University), Prof. Amitav Acharya (American University) Prof. Joel S. Migdal (Washington University), Prof. Andrew G. Walder (Stanford University),Prof. Susan Shirk (UC San Diego) have joined our school to offer courses and lectures.

  • Prof. Peter J. Katzenstein (Cornell University)
  • Prof. Peter J. Katzenstein (Cornell University)

Many more distinguished have joined us from other parts of the worlds, for example, Prof. Hans van de Ven (Cambridge University), Prof. John Dunn (Cambridge University), prof. Richard J. Evans (Cambridge University), Prof. Rana Mitter (Oxford University), and Prof. MacGregor Knox (London School of Economics and Political Science).

Our faculty, students and staff are eager to continue to welcome experts from across the globe to foster mutual understanding and exchange perspectives.

As the programs vary from year to year, interested professors can contact the school (rucsis@ruc.edu.cn) for the latest information.
